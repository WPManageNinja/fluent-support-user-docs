# Close Ticket Silently

Fluent Support has an automatic ticket-closing feature that notifies the users via email when their ticket is closed. But, the **Close Ticket Silently** feature allows the support agents to close tickets silently **without sending any email notification to the user**. This article will guide you through the whole process of doing it.

## Close Ticket Silently 

To learn how you can **close** your **ticket silently** using Fluent Support, follow the steps with the screenshots below â€”

First, go to your **Fluent Support** and click on **Tickets**. Then go to your desired ticket section from **My Tickets, All Tickets, Unassigned,** or **Bookmarks**

Open the specific ticket you want to close silently and click on the **Three dot Icon**.  
Now, select **Close Ticket Silently** and a confirmation message (Ticket has been closed) will appear that only you can see.

You can also see that the **Closed Ticket** if you return to this ticket.
