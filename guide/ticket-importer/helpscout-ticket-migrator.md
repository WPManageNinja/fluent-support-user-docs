# HelpScout Ticket Migrator

[Original Documentation](https://fluentsupport.com/docs/helpscout-ticket-migrator/)

---

[**HelpScout**](https://www.helpscout.com/) is a SaaS tool for customer communication. **HelpScout** users can easily transfer their **tickets** to [**Fluent Support**](https://fluentsupport.com/) through an **API**. This article will guide you through the process.

## Import Tickets From Help Scout Into Fluent Support #

To learn how to import tickets from **Help Scout** into **Fluent Support** , follow the steps with screenshots below —

From the **Fluent Support Dashboard** , go to **Settings** or **Global Settings** , select the **Ticket Importer** , find **Help Scout** for ticket migration, and click **Import Tickets**.

![Ticket migration from HelpScout](public/images/ticket-importer/helpscout-ticket-migrator/dashboard-helpscout-import-ticket-scaled-1.webp)

A popup page will appear asking for your **App ID** , **App** **Secret** , and **Authorization Code**. Now, copy the **HelpScout Redirection URL** for future use.

![HelpScout Redirection URL](public/images/ticket-importer/helpscout-ticket-migrator/help-scout-popup-page-scaled-1.webp)

To get the **App ID** and **Secret,** go to your [**HelpScout**](https://www.helpscout.com/) account. Go to the **Profile Icon** from the right-hand corner of the **Dashboard,** select **Your Profile** , choose **My Apps,** and click the **Create My App** button.

![Create My App in HelpScout](public/images/ticket-importer/helpscout-ticket-migrator/profile-icon-my-apps-create-app-scaled-1.webp)

Name your App, paste the previously copied URL into the **Redirection URL** field, and click the **Create** button.

![Paste Redirection URL](public/images/ticket-importer/helpscout-ticket-migrator/app-name-redirection-url-create-scaled-1.webp)

Now, copy the **App ID** and **App Secret** from here.

![App ID and App Secret](public/images/ticket-importer/helpscout-ticket-migrator/copy-app-id-app-secret-scaled-1.webp)

Return to the **Help Scout** popup page from the **Fluent Support Dashboard** and paste the **App ID** & **App Secret** copied from your Help Scout account into the desired field.

![Import tickets from HelpScout popup in Fluent Support dashboard](public/images/ticket-importer/helpscout-ticket-migrator/paste-app-id-app-secret-scaled-1.webp)

To obtain the Authorization Code, click the **Get Authorized** button now.

![ Get Authorized option](public/images/ticket-importer/helpscout-ticket-migrator/get-authorized-button-scaled-1.webp)

It will bring you back to the **HelpScout** page, now click on the **Authorize** button.

![HelpScout page](public/images/ticket-importer/helpscout-ticket-migrator/authorize-button-1.webp)

You will receive your Authorization code here, now simply copy it.

![Receive your Authorization code](public/images/ticket-importer/helpscout-ticket-migrator/copy-authorization-code-scaled-1.webp)

Return to the **Help Scout** popup page again and paste this in the **Authorization Code** field.

![Authorization Code field](public/images/ticket-importer/helpscout-ticket-migrator/paste-the-authorization-code-scaled-1.webp)

Click the **Request Token** to get your **Access Token**. Your all mailboxes will appear in the dropdown list under the **Choose Mailbox** field. Select the required mailbox to import tickets.

You can choose only one **Mailbox** at a time, and without selecting the specific mailbox you won’t be able to import your tickets.

![Request Token option](public/images/ticket-importer/helpscout-ticket-migrator/request-ticket-button-choose-mailbox-scaled-1.webp)

Once you are done, click the **Import Tickets** button.

![Finally Import Tickets ](public/images/ticket-importer/helpscout-ticket-migrator/finally-import-tickets-button-scaled-1.webp)

Congratulations, you are now ready to proceed! The importation of tickets will begin, and a notification will be sent once all your tickets have been successfully imported.

Here, you can see all the **Tickets** are **Imported** and the migration date & time are displayed as well.

![Preview of Imported Tickets from HelpScout](public/images/ticket-importer/helpscout-ticket-migrator/help-scout-tickets-migrated-1.webp)

This is how you can migrate your all tickets from **Help Scout** to **Fluent Support**!
