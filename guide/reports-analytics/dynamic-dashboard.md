# Dynamic Dashboard

Fluent Support has an impressive new **Dynamic Dashboard** for user convenience. With this dynamic feature, customers can effortlessly customize their dashboard by adding or removing information as needed. To get an overview of the **Dynamic Dashboard** of **Fluent Support** , read the article accordingly.

## An Overview Of Dynamic Dashboard

Right now, the **Dynamic dashboard** has **three functionalities** and these are:

  * **Drag & Drop**
  * **Settings**
  * **Reset**

### Drag & Drop 

With the Drag & Drop feature, all the widget sections of the [General Dashboard](/guide/getting-started/dashboard-overview) can be moved according to your requirements. You can simply click and hold a widget header, then drag it to your desired position within the grid layout.

![Drag & Drop option for the General Dashboard](/guide/public/images/reports-analytics/dynamic-dashboard/drag-drop.gif)

### Settings

You will find the **Settings (Gear) Icon** in the top right corner of your **Fluent Support Dashboard**. This option allows you to customize your dashboard interface via a dedicated sidebar panel.

Specifically, from the **All Widgets** sidebar, you can:

 * **Search:** Use the search bar to find specific widgets quickly.
 * **Add Widgets:** Click the **Add** button next to any available widget to display it on your dashboard.
 * **View Status:** See which widgets are currently Added to your view.

![Settings option to customize your Dashboard](/guide/public/images/reports-analytics/dynamic-dashboard/settings-1-1.gif)

### Reset 

You will also find the **Reset** button right next to the **Settings** icon. This allows you to instantly undo all custom changes and revert the dashboard back to its default layout and widget configuration.

![Reset option for undo all changes of your Dashboard](/guide/public/images/reports-analytics/dynamic-dashboard/reset-2-1.gif)

This is how simple it is to customize your dashboard dynamically! You can also check out, our [Dashboard Overview](/guide/getting-started/dashboard-overview).
