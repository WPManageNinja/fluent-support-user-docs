# Dynamic Dashboard

Fluent Support has an impressive new **Dynamic Dashboard** for user convenience. With this dynamic feature, customers can effortlessly customize their dashboard by adding or removing information as needed. To get an overview of the **Dynamic Dashboard** of **Fluent Support** , read the article accordingly.

## An Overview Of Dynamic Dashboard

Right now, the **Dynamic dashboard** has **three functionalities** and these are:

  * **Drag & Drop**
  * **Settings**
  * **Reset**

### Drag & Drop 

With this **Drag & Drop**, all the sections & their categories of the [General Dashboard](https://fluentsupport.com/docs/dashboard-overview/) can be draggable according to your requirements.

![Drag & Drop option for the General Dashboard](/guide/public/images/reports-analytics/dynamic-dashboard/drag-drop.gif)

### Settings

Youâ€™ll find the **Settings** icon in the top right corner of your **Fluent Support Dashboard**. This option allows you to customize your dashboard interface. Specifically, you can choose which sections to display and which to hide on your dashboard page.

![Settings option to customize your Dashboard](/guide/public/images/reports-analytics/dynamic-dashboard/settings-1-1.gif)

### Reset 

You will also find the **Reset** button right next to the **Settings** icon. This allows you to undo all the changes that you have previously made.

![Reset option for undo all changes of your Dashboard](/guide/public/images/reports-analytics/dynamic-dashboard/reset-2-1.gif)

This is how simple it is to customize your dashboard dynamically! You can also check out, our [Dashboard Overview](https://fluentsupport.com/docs/dashboard-overview/).
