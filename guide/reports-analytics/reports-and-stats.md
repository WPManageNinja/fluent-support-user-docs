# Reports and Stats

The **“Reports”** is an advanced statistical analytics and reporting feature of Fluent Support. It provides an accurate view of the entire team’s performance and even allows you to track and measure the performance of individual support executives. This article will give you a focused overview of the dashboard in the **“Reports”** feature.

## Accessing Reports

You will find the **Reports** option in the top navigation menu of your **Fluent Support Dashboard**. This will display **six** different sections of ticket statistics and reports:

  * [Personal Reports](/guide/reports-analytics/personal-report)
  * [Agents Reports](/guide/reports-analytics/agents-report)
  * [Products Reports](/guide/reports-analytics/products-report)
  * [Business Boxes Reports](/guide/reports-analytics/business-boxes-reports)
  * [Activity Reports](/guide/reports-analytics/activity-reports)
  * [Time Sheet](/guide/reports-analytics/time-sheet)
